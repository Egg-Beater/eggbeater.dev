  • What is Merangue?

    Merangue is a google workspace extension that unites google services and implements project and content management features on top of tools you already know and rely on.

  • I have private or sensitive data how can I be sure Merangue will keep my data secure?

    Merangue follows all of the same standards and practices implemented in the Google products you already trust and use everyday in fact they are required for our Firebase back end to function. We are also compliant with all GDPR regulations.

  • How much is it going to cost me and my organization to use?

    Instead of following a traditional SaaS pricing model we decided to make Merangue far more cost effective for small to medium size businesses' by offering both bundle packages as well as a menu of a'la carte product offerings if that is what best suits your business or budget.

  • What requirements does an installation of Merangue have?

    Th only requirements for a full installation are any tier google workspace subscription and the approval of an admin level installation by any admin of that workspace.
