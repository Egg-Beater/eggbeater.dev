Application recipes for efficient workflows, no heat  required.

Merangue is a custom-built Google Workspace application that blends automation functionalities with integrations into other google services you are already familiar with such as calendars, sheets, mail, as well as other external services. Merangue is more than just a set of software tools, think of it as a life jacket keeping your head above the waves of anxiety that come with an increasingly interconnected and digitally demanding lifestyle required to succeed as one of this generation's entrepreneurs. Merangue takes the technologies we already rely on to get things done and whisks them together into an environment where anyone who contributes can easily get what they need to work as they do best, while eliminating the endless meetings, chaotic scheduling, and poor communication which diminish the capability of small teams.
